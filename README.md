# My personal website :O
It's a website for me to post my projects, my socials, a little about me, my blog, and a part for ideas :D

## Why did i build this?
I had just finished the OBS macropad project and i wanted to do some other project for #macondo, i had the idea to create a website the would serve as my portfolio at first it would have a website that would ask if your doing good and then it would lead you onto the real website, but i scraped that idea because it was too complicated for the moment, so i just went and made a website that would show a about me, a blog, my socials, my projects and my youtube videos, but putting the youtube videos was too plain so i thought, hmmmm waht about an idea/suggestion for the site, my channel and projects, so i did, first i finished everything, then changed the youtube part to the idea thingy and then in the middle of doing it though again, hmmmmmmmmm, what about brazilian people (bc its my portfolio so duhh ;-;), tehn i made a translated version that works on every subpage, after all that i made the idea part, the one that i've spent most hours on, like 5 hrs just for the js ;-;. Right now we only have that but in the future i will update it :D

## How it works (for the idea page!)
Works on both translated versions, and you jsut put you name, choose your country and describe your idea, then hit the send button, idea will be reviewed in a week or less and will be there if it was approved :D
