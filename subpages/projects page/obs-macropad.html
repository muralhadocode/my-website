The OBS Macropad (my first hardware project :D)


Basically a macropad is a mini keyboard wich do pre-programmed things like a shortcut or a specific thing for you, so this macropad has the function to help streamers and youtuber when doing a video or a livestream, the macropad can stop and start a recording and stop and start a livestream, it can change the scenes in OBS and regulate the volume of the microphone and the desktop. It sounds basic and simple (because it is 🕊), but can make the process of making videos and streaming much easier for me and other mini-youtubers, you can see the entire thing in this repository.
